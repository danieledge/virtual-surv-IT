Method (per parameter)

  • off_market_threshold_bps - the natural unit is deviation ÷ prevailing spread (spread-normalised), not flat bps. Set per liquidity segment where the true-positive ratio distribution separates from the FP distribution (sweep, P5-TP subject to P95-FP below it).
  • lookback_days - P90-P95 of the legitimate same-UBO round-trip lag.
  • ubo_graph_max_age_days - a data-quality gate, not a stat threshold: the UBO feed refresh SLA + a grace day (confirm with the data owner).
  • min_notional (required, currently absent - QA DEF-003) - a de-minimis floor; an immaterial-notional pair can't give a "false or misleading signal" under MAR Art 12(1)(a). A compliance risk-appetite decision, not pure stats.

Volume↔coverage trade-off

Tightening off-market catches at-market evasion but floods on affiliated-fund flow; loosening cuts load but blinds the scenario to near-market washes. Two highest-value moves: add min_notional (precision gain, negligible coverage cost) and switch to spread-normalised thresholds per segment (improves precision in illiquid names and recall in liquid ones simultaneously).
