1. Context & regulatory driver

Detect layering (MAR Art. 12(1)(a); FCA MW69 expects typology- and asset-class-specific calibration): non-bona-fide orders on one side of the book to move the touch, with genuine executions on the opposite side.

2. Scope

Equities on venue set XLON/XETR only (Round 2 decision - see register). Order-level data including cancels/amends; out of scope: cross-product spoof-and-trade (tracked as a watch item for a TS-003).
